Lemma 5.9. Converging Distances.

If \(X\) is a metric space and \((x_n)_{n=1}^\infty,(y_n)_{n=1}^\infty\subseteq X\) converge, then
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} d(\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}x_n,\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}y_n)=\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}d(x_n,y_n) \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
