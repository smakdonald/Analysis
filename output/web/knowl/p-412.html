Let \(X\) be a metric space, let \(S\subseteq X\) be a subset, and view \(S\) as a metric space with the induced metric. Then \(S\) is disconnected iff exist open sets \(U,V\subseteq X\) such that \(S\subseteq U\cup V,\) \(U\cap V=\emptyset\text{,}\) \(S\cap U\neq \emptyset,\) and \(S\cap V\neq \emptyset\)
