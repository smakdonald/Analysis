Corollary 6.12. Lower and Upper Darboux Integrals.

For any \(f\in\mathcal{B}[a,b]\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} \underline{\int_a^b}f(t)\,dt\leq \overline{\int_a^b}f(t)\,dt. \end{equation*}
In particular, \(f\in\mathcal{R}[a,b]\iff\)
\begin{equation*} \underline{\int_a^b}f(t)\,dt\geq \overline{\int_a^b}f(t)\,dt. \end{equation*}
