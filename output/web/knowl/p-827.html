Let \(M<\infty\) and \(\mathcal{F} \subseteq \mathcal{C}([a, b])\) be given. Assume that each \(f \in \mathcal{F}\) is differentiable on \((a, b)\) and satisfies \(|f(a)| \leq M\) and \(\left|f^{\prime}(x)\right| \leq M\) for all \(x \in(a, b)\text{.}\) Prove that \(\mathcal{F}\) is equicontinuous on \([a, b]\text{.}\)
