Let \(\alpha, M>0\) be given, and suppose that \(\left\{f_{n}\right\}_{n \geq 1}\) satisfies \(\left|f_{n}(x)-f_{n}(y)\right| \leq M|x-y|^{\alpha}\text{,}\) for all \(n \geq 1\) and all \(x, y\) in an interval \([a, b]\text{.}\) Show that \(\left\{f_{n}\right\}_{n \geq 1}\) is equicontinuous.
