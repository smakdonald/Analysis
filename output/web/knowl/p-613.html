If \(f,g:[a,b]\rightarrow\mathbb{R}\) and \(\alpha\in\mathbb{R}\text{,}\) then
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} V_{[a,b]}(\alpha f)&=\left|{\alpha}\right|V_{[a,b]}(f);\\ V_{[a,b]}(f+g)&\leq V_{[a,b]}(f)+V_{[a,b]}(g). \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
In particular, \(\mathcal{BV}[a,b]\) is a vector space.
