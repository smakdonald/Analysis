Item 2.

Let \(\left\{q_{n}\right\}_{n=1}^{\infty} \subset[0,1]\) be an enumeration of \(\mathbb{Q} \cap[0,1]\text{,}\) the rational numbers in \([0,1]\text{.}\) Define \(\beta:[0,1] \rightarrow \mathbb{R}\) by
\begin{equation*} \beta(x):= \begin{cases}\frac{1}{n}, & x=q_{n}, \\ 0, & x \notin \mathbb{Q} \cap[0,1].\end{cases} \end{equation*}
Prove or disprove: \(\beta\) has bounded variation on \([0,1]\text{.}\)
