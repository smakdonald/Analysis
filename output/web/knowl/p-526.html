If \(S\subseteq \mathbb{R},a\in S\) is an interior point, \(f,g:S\rightarrow\mathbb{R}\) are differentiable at \(a\) and \(\alpha\in\mathbb{R}\text{,}\) then
  1. \((f+g)'(a)=f'(a)+g'(a)\text{;}\)
  2. \((\alpha f)'(a)=\alpha f'(a)\text{;}\)
  3. \((fg)'(a)=f'(a)g(a)+f(a)g'(a)\text{;}\)
  4. \(\left({\frac{f}{g}}\right)'(a)=\frac{g(a)f'(a)-f(a)g'(a)}{(g(a))^2}\) if \(g(a)\neq 0\text{.}\)
