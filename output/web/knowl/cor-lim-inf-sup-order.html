Corollary 2.35.

If \((x_n)_{n=1}^{\infty}\subseteq \mathbb{R}\) is a sequence and \((x_{n_k})_{k=1}^{\infty}\subseteq \mathbb{R}\text{,}\) then
\begin{equation*} \liminf_{n\rightarrow\infty}x_{n}\leq \liminf_{k\rightarrow\infty}x_{n_k}\leq \limsup_{k\rightarrow\infty}x_{n_k}\leq \limsup_{n\rightarrow\infty}x_{n} \end{equation*}

Proof.
