Theorem 6.17. Integral Algebra.

If \(f,g\in\mathcal{B}[a,b],c\in\mathbb{R}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle \overline{\int_a^b}(f+g)(t)\,dt\leq \overline{\int_a^b}f(t)\,dt+\overline{\int_a^b}g(t)\,dt\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \overline{\int_a^b}cf(t)\,dt=c\overline{\int_a^b}f(t)\,dt\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \underline{\int_a^b}(f+g)(t)\,dt\geq \underline{\int_a^b}f(t)\,dt+\underline{\int_a^b}g(t)\,dt\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \underline{\int_a^b}cf(t)\,dt=c\underline{\int_a^b}f(t)\,dt\)
