Problem A.39.

\begin{equation*} \sigma(x, y):=\min \{1, \rho(x, y)\}. \end{equation*}
  1. Prove that \(\sigma\) is a metric on \(X\text{.}\)
  2. Prove that \((X, \rho)\) is complete if and only if \((X, \sigma)\) is complete.
