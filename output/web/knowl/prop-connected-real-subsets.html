Proposition 4.73. Connected Real Subsets.

If \(S\subseteq \mathbb{R}\) is a nonempty connected set, then either
  • \(\displaystyle S=(a,b),\exists -\infty\leq a<b\leq \infty\)
  • \(\displaystyle S=(a,b],\exists -\infty\leq a<b< \infty\)
  • \(\displaystyle S=[a,b),\exists -\infty< a<b\leq \infty\)
  • \(\displaystyle S=[a,b],\exists -\infty< a<b< \infty\)
